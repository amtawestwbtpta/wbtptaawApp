Understanding WB Leave Salary Calculator for West Bengal Government Employees

The WB Leave Salary Calculator is a powerful tool designed to help West Bengal government employees estimate their Earned Leave (EL) encashment. Whether you're planning retirement or simply want to understand the financial benefits of your unused leave, this calculator simplifies the process.

How Does the WB Leave Salary Calculator Work?

The calculator uses a straightforward formula: ((Basic Pay + Dearness Allowance) × Number of ELs) ÷ 30. You input your Basic Pay, Dearness Allowance (DA), and the number of Earned Leaves you wish to encash. The tool ensures that the number of ELs does not exceed 300, as per West Bengal government regulations.

Important Notes for West Bengal Govt Employees

Before using the calculator, keep these points in mind:

  • Maximum EL Limit: Only up to 300 Earned Leaves can be encashed.
  • Inputs: Ensure your Basic Pay and DA are up-to-date, as these directly impact the result.
  • Taxes: The calculated amount may be subject to tax deductions, which are not included in this tool.
  • Verification: Contact your DDO or Accountant General (A&E), West Bengal, for balance verification or form submission.
